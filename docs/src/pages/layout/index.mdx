  </p>
  <p>
    The top navbar is a standard Bootstrap
    <code>nav.navbar.navbar-expand-lg</code> with an additional class of

    <a href="https://getbootstrap.com/docs/4.6/components/navbar/">

    </a>

  </p>
  <p>
    The <code>.modus-header</code> should be followed by
    <code>.modus-body</code> to set up the left nav area and main content area.
  </p>
  <p>
    After <code>.modus-body</code> should be
    <code>nav.modus-sidebar</code> which contains a vertical nav list (more info
    below).
  </p>
  <p>
    The next sibling element after the <code>.modus-sidebar</code> should be
    <code>.modus-content-rows</code> with an optional child of
    <code>.modus-toolbar</code>.
  </p>
  <p>
    After <code>.modus-content-rows</code> should be
    <code>.modus-content-columns</code> with an optional child of
    <code>.modus-panel</code>.
  </p>
  <p>
    Your main content is housed within <code>.modus-content</code> inside of
    <code>.modus-content-columns</code>.
  </p>
  <p>
    After the <code>.modus-body</code>, the next element should be
    <code>.modus-footer</code>.
